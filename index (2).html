About Me

Hello, i`m Vex / Arfi! I’m a passionate and self-taught programmer who thrives on creativity, innovation, and continuous learning. My main focus is on scripting and web development, with a strong command of LuaU, JavaScript/Node.js, HTML, and CSS. These languages are the tools I use to bring ideas to life, whether inside a Roblox game or on a modern website. I began my programming journey through curiosity and experimentation. Over time, it grew into a true passion as I started building complex scripts, automating systems, and developing clean user interfaces. In the world of LuaU, I specialize in developing custom Roblox scripts, game enhancement tools, and exploit-proof mechanics. I enjoy working with game logic, real-time interactions, UI scripting, and server-to-client communication. My projects often focus on performance, user control, and smart automation to enhance the in-game experience. On the web development side, I work with HTML and CSS to create beautiful, responsive layouts. I love pushing the limits of design, using modern CSS techniques and animations to build websites that feel alive and interactive. I combine this with JavaScript to create dynamic interfaces, and I use Node.js for backend functionality, whether it’s handling requests, managing data, or integrating APIs. My web development projects are often built with mobile responsiveness, speed optimization, and user experience in mind. What sets me apart is my commitment to exploring the unknown. I enjoy learning how systems work from the inside out, whether it's decoding obfuscated scripts, analyzing server-client logic, or building full-stack solutions. I'm always pushing myself to go further, building my own tools, libraries, or frameworks to solve unique problems. I believe in writing clean, maintainable, and scalable code. Whether it's a small utility script or a full web application, I pay close attention to structure, logic, and user flow. I also value collaboration, feedback, and the opportunity to learn from others. I’m constantly updating my skills and exploring new technologies to stay ahead of the curve. Outside of programming, I’m someone who values growth, consistency, and discipline. I enjoy setting personal goals, whether it's completing long-term challenges, improving focus, or learning something new each day. My journey as a developer isn’t just about code—it’s about building the mindset and habits needed to solve problems, create value, and innovate in the digital world. I’m always open to exciting projects, collaborations, or learning opportunities. Whether you're looking for someone to bring a game idea to life, build a sleek website, or solve a technical challenge, I’m ready to take it on with creativity and passion.

Skilled in LuaU, JavaScript/Node.js, HTML, and CSS, I enjoy turning creative ideas and technical challenges into efficient, user-focused solutions.
